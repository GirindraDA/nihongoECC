- eigo ga dekimasen

? eigo ga dekimasuka
=o hai, dekimasu
=x iie, dekimasen

- (watashi wa) ryori ga tokui jyanaidesu

? nani ga tokui desuka

  donna ryori ga tokui desuka
  = nihon ryori ga tokui desu	


For 'verbs'
+ uta wo utau koto ga tokui desu

- uta wo utau koto ga tokui jyanai desu

=o uta wo utau koto ga tokui desu

? donna uta wo utau koto ga tokui desuka
