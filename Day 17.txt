Kanazawa WA kenrokuen GA yuumei desu. SOREKARA .... MO yuumei desu.

ryoukan ni tomari masenka?
ee, tomari mashou

+ ryokan arata ni shimasu
? dono ryokan ni shimasuka

kanazawa ryokan ka ryoka arata ga ii desu

? kanazawa ryokan TO ryoka arata TO DOCHIRA GA yasui desuka.
+ DOCHIRA MO yasui desu.

= ryokan arata no hou ga yasui desu KARA, yokan arata NI simashou.

Conversation 2:

1.
A : ryokan ni tomari masenka
B : ee, tomari mashou. dono ryokan ni simasuka?
A : kanazawa ryokan ka ryokan arata ga ii desu.
B : kanazwa ryokan to ryokan arata to dochira ga benri desuka?
A :  ryokan arata no hou ga benri desu
B : ryokan arata no hou ga benri desu kara, ryokan arata ni simashou.

2.
A : sushi wo tabemasenka
B : ee, tabemasho. dono sushiya ga ii desuka?
A : sushi taishyou ka sushi ichiba ga ii desu
B : sushi taishou to sushi ichiba to dochira ga yasui desuka
A : sushi taishou no hou ga yasui desu
B : sushi taishou no hou ga yasui desu kara, sushi taishou ni simashou

3.
A : kohi wo nomimasenka?
B : ee, nomimashou. dono kafe ga ii desuka?
A : kafe fururu ka kafe rujyu ga ii desu
B : kafe fururu to kafe rujyu to dochira ga osyare desuka?
A : kafe fururu no hou ga osyare desu
B : kafe fururu no hou ga osyare desu kara, kafe fururu ni shimashou

4.
A : jitensha wo karimasenka?
B : ee, karimashou. dono jitensha ya ga ii desuka?

B : jitensha ya to saikuringu ueki to dochira ga eki kara chikai desuka?
A : saikuringu ueki no hou ga eki kara chikai desu.
B : saikuringu ueki no hou ga eki kara chikai desu kara, saikuringu ueki ni shimashou
