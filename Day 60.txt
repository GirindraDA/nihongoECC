Listening 1:

1. atami no besso desu
2. atami kara hanabi kai tai mirareu kamosirmasen

1. nagame = YOI
- atami no hanabi taikai ga (mirareru)kamosiremasen.
2. nedan = YOI
- sonna ni (takakunai) noni hatake mo onsen mo arimasu
3. kaimono = YOKUNAI
- eki kara (tooi) kara, kaimono ga (taihen) kamosiremasen
4. tenki = YOKUNAI
- fuyu ha samuku (kanjiru) kamosiremasen.


Conversation 1:

1.
A: koko ha nedan ha sonna ni takakunai noni, hatake mo onsen mo atte ii desune.
B: hai, sou desune. demo, eki kara tooi kara, kaimono ga taihen kamosiremasen ne.

2.
A: kono ie ha chiku 30 nen na noni, daikan mo heya mo kirei desune
B: hai sou desune. demo, minami muki dewa nai kara, hi atari ga yokunai kamosiremasen

3.
A: koko ha ninki na eria na noni, yachin ga yasui desune. 
B: hai, sou desune. ima ha gakusei ga heya wo sagasite iru jiki dakara hayaku keiyaku sita hou ga ii kamosiremasen ne.

4.
A: kyuujyou kara hanareta tokoro  aru noni ouen no koe ya otto ga yoku kikoemasune.
B: hai sou desune. mawari ga shizuka dakara yoru osoku made siai ga attara urusaku kanjiru kamosiremasen
